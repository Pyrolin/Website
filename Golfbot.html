Golf Bot

Project Overview: This project was made for the DTU course CDIO. As a group we were tasked with creating a Lego mindstorm robot, that by looking at a webcam, is able to collect and deposit table tennis balls, while also dodging obstacles. All of the robots would then be tested at the end and 1 of them declared the winner.

Goal: The goal of this project was to show that we could concieve, develop, implement and operate a project.

My Role & Contributions: Team management, coding of movement, computer vision.

Technologies Used: Python, Git, ev3-micropython 2.0.0.

Challenges & Solutions: Our computer vision initially had problems with differentiating the different objects on the course. This was solved by implementing a grid function, so that instead of looking on the field as a whole, the robot instead looks at all of the different grids, which gave it much more precise vision.

Results: We ended up getting 5th place in the final competition, which is something we were very happy with. In the end we got a robot that is able to collect balls and deliver them all the while dodgeing obstacles.
